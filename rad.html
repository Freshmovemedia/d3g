What We Do

D3G has 25 years of experience with HUD, RAD, FHA, Section 8 PBRA/PBV, LIHTC and mixed finance real estate repositioning services. Our program access and knowledge of HUD electronic tools (e-CNA & HEROS) combined with early planning transactional guidance from knowledgeable, in-house affordable housing professionals is why you want D3G as your RAD guide.

How we can help

We’ve supported more than 1,000 successful RAD projects and completed over 900 CNA e-tools and 500 environmental/NEPA/HEROS Reports for RAD. Four out of the five largest Public Housing Agencies in the country have chosen D3G as their due diligence provider and consultant. We’ve also had great success working with some of the smallest PHA’s too.
